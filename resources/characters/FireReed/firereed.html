FireReed

Kvothe, a prodigy in the arcane arts. From a young age he was able to feel the arcane energies around him, surrounding him like air. While heir to his household, Kvothe had no patience or tact for the politics involved. Instead, he decided to go to the Arcanum, and learn all he could from the meisters. A few years after, he graduated from the Arcanum and returned home, only to find that a huge party was being thrown in his honor. With a cloudy vision he wakes up, amongst bodies burnt to a crips, broken columns and bloody floors. He knows what his body did, he remembers what his hands did, and yet, he could not stop it...
